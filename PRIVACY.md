# UMAX Privacy Policy

*Last updated: April 17, 2026*

UMAX ("we", "our", "the app") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information the app handles and how.

## 1. Photos & face analysis

All face analysis in UMAX runs **locally on your device** using the Google ML Kit Face Detection SDK. Your selfies:

- Are **never** uploaded to our servers.
- Are **never** shared with any third party.
- Are stored on your device only, inside the app's private sandbox.
- Can be deleted at any time by removing the scan in the **Progress** tab or uninstalling the app.

## 2. What we collect

| Data | Where it lives | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Your age, gender, goal | Local device only | Calibrate trait scoring & routine recommendations |
| Scan history (photos + scores) | Local device only | Let you see your glow-up over time |
| Subscription/entitlement flags | Local device only | Unlock paid features after purchase |

**We do not maintain user accounts.** We do not sync data to the cloud. We cannot see any of the above.

## 3. Advertising identifiers (AdMob)

When you use UMAX's free tier, Google AdMob may collect advertising identifiers to serve ads. You can limit this in your OS settings:

- **iOS** — Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising → toggle Personalized Ads off, or decline the App Tracking Transparency prompt.
- **Android** — Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

Google's ad data practices are governed by their [Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## 4. In-app purchases

Purchases are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We receive only a transaction identifier and product ID — we never see your payment details.

## 5. Children

UMAX is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13.

## 6. Your rights

- **Delete your data**: uninstall the app. All local data is removed.
- **Opt out of ads personalization**: use the OS-level toggles above.
- **Contact us**: nalhamzy@gmail.com

## 7. Changes

We'll update this page when the policy materially changes. The "Last updated" date above reflects the current version.
